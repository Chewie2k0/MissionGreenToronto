Trees Against Inequity

  • Project Location: Weston and Parkdale neighbourhoods
  • Partners: Tree Mobile
  • Project date: May 14/15 2022
  • Sponsored by: Government of Canada, Canada Service Corps, TakingITGlobal, Irving Tissues

Trees Against Inequity

Unfortunately, all neighbourhoods in Toronto do not have the same amount of tree cover. Trees provide vital ecosystem services to the community such as stormwater management and air regulation and helps reduce the urban heat island effect. Residential properties offer the most potential spaces for trees to be planted in neighbourhoods. Mission Green Toronto hopes to tap into this resource by offering homeowners in low-canopy neighbourhoods free tree-planting opportunities.

The neighbourhoods we have chosen to work in for our 2022 Project are
- York - South Weston
- Parkdale

MGT would like to thank its tree-planting partner, Tree Mobile Toronto, for its great support during the project. Thank you to the Government of Canada, Canada Service Corps, TakingITGlobal, and Irving Tissues for supporting and getting this project off the ground!
