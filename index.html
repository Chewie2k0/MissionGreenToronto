Who we are

We are a youth urban forestry group that aspires to help expand the city's urban forest and increase our communities' engagement with nature

Improve Tree Cover

We hope to increase the number of trees in neighbourhoods with low canopy cover by providing free tree planting opportunities to residential homeowners and other private property owners.

Increase environmental awareness

Increase environmental awareness in neighbourhoods and promote social bonding between businesses and residents

Community Engagement

Empowering communities and local residents to actively engage with their urban forests and develop and carry out their own initiatives

Our Mission

To engage with the people of Toronto and help them embrace the urban forest

Our Vision

Aspiring to help create a healthier, equitable and more widespread urban forest in Toronto

Call To Action

We are currently offering homeowners in low-canopy neighbourhoods free tree-planting opportunities. If you would like to apply, please contact us below.
